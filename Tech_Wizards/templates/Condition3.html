Condition: Brown Spots

Brown Spots

Symptoms:

  • Small brown spots that appear on leaves, stems, and panicles.
  • Spots that grow to be oval or circular, and can coalesce into large patches.
  • Brown or black spots on glumes.
  • Discolored seeds.
  • Dead seedlings.
  • Dark coffee-colored spots in the panicles.

Cure:

  • Spray propiconazole during the booting to heading stages.
  • Use Trichoderma spp. as a biocontrol agent.
  • Use salicylic acid or benzoic acid.
  • Use the chemical fungicide Hinosan.
